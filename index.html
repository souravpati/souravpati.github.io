About

I am a Software Development Engineer at Amazon based in Seattle, Washington. I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Southern California, where I focused on applying mathematical modeling and algorithmic principles to build intelligent computing systems.

My work spans scalable distributed systems, real-time architectures, and applied machine learning. I have experience with large language models (LLMs), graph processing frameworks, and high-performance computing. I am passionate about solving complex engineering challenges and building systems that operate reliably at scale.

Outside of work, I have a background in competitive programming, having represented my university at ACM ICPC Asia Regionals. I also enjoy chess and have competed at the state level.
